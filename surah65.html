Surah At-Talaq – The Divorce

Classification: Madani | Verses: 12

Surah At-Talaq, revealed in Madinah, provides guidance on family law, specifically regarding divorce, maintenance, and treatment of spouses. It outlines the rules for issuing a divorce, the waiting period ('iddah), and the responsibility of husbands to provide for their divorced wives during this period. The surah emphasizes obedience to Allah’s commands, trust in His provision, and the importance of fairness and justice in domestic matters. It also encourages reliance on Allah, adherence to moral conduct, and reflection on the consequences of one’s actions, both in this life and the Hereafter.

  • Provides detailed guidance on divorce, waiting periods, and maintenance.
  • Stresses fairness, justice, and ethical treatment of spouses.
  • Encourages obedience to Allah’s commands in family and social matters.
  • Highlights reliance on Allah and trust in His provision.
  • Warns of accountability for actions and moral responsibility.
  • Encourages reflection on consequences of deeds in both this life and the Hereafter.
