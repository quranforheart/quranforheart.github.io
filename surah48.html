Surah Al-Fath – The Victory

Classification: Madani | Verses: 29

Surah Al-Fath, revealed in Madinah, is the forty-eighth chapter of the Quran. It celebrates the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah, which marked a peaceful victory for the Muslims, demonstrating Allah’s favor and planning. The surah emphasizes faith, obedience, patience, and trust in Allah’s wisdom. It reassures believers of divine support, forgiveness, and the promise of future victories. The chapter also highlights the qualities of true believers and the importance of loyalty to the Prophet (ﷺ) and his mission.

  • Commemorates the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah as a clear sign of Allah’s favor and guidance.
  • Encourages patience, trust, and obedience to Allah and His Messenger.
  • Promises forgiveness, spiritual reward, and future victories to sincere believers.
  • Highlights the qualities of true believers: steadfastness, loyalty, and readiness to strive in Allah’s cause.
  • Warns hypocrites and those who oppose the Prophet (ﷺ) of consequences.
  • Encourages unity and cooperation within the Muslim community for achieving success.
  • Emphasizes Allah’s ultimate control over all affairs and His wisdom in planning events.
  • Reassures the Prophet (ﷺ) and the believers that their efforts are guided and supported by Allah.
