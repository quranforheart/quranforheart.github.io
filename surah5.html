Surah Al-Ma'idah – The Table Spread

Classification: Madani

Verses: 120

Surah Al-Ma’idah, revealed in Madinah, emphasizes fulfilling covenants and obeying Allah’s commands. It provides detailed guidance on social, ethical, and religious responsibilities, including dietary laws, lawful and prohibited actions, and proper conduct within the Muslim community. The surah addresses the People of the Book, inviting them to follow true guidance while warning against hypocrisy, disobedience, and altering divine teachings. It stresses justice, fairness, and accountability, encouraging believers to uphold righteousness even in difficult situations. Themes of unity, cooperation in goodness, and moral responsibility are highlighted, along with reminders from the experiences of previous nations who ignored divine guidance. Overall, the surah reinforces faith, ethical living, communal harmony, and obedience to Allah.
