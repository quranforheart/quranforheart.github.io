Surah Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread)

Classification: Madani

Verses: 120

Summary

Surah Al-Ma’idah emphasizes fulfilling covenants and adhering to Allah’s commands. It provides guidance on ethical and social responsibilities, including dietary laws, proper conduct, and respect for agreements. The surah addresses the People of the Book, inviting them to follow true guidance while warning against hypocrisy and disobedience. Key themes include justice, accountability, moral conduct, cooperation in righteousness, and lessons from previous nations’ experiences. Overall, it reinforces faith, unity, and ethical living according to divine guidance.
