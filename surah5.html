Surah Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread)

Classification: Madani

Verses: 120

Summary

Surah Al-Ma’idah focuses on the importance of fulfilling covenants and obeying Allah’s commands. It outlines dietary laws, emphasizes justice, and warns against hypocrisy. The surah also addresses the People of the Book, inviting them to follow the true guidance and reminding them of the prophets’ messages. It stresses accountability, ethical behavior, and the importance of cooperation in righteousness.
