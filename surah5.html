Surah Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread)

Classification: Madani

Verses: 120

Expanded Summary

Surah Al-Ma’idah emphasizes the importance of fulfilling covenants and following Allah’s commands. It provides guidelines to maintain justice and harmony in the Muslim community. The surah addresses lawful and prohibited actions, social conduct, and accountability for personal and collective behavior.

Key themes include:

  • Lawful and Prohibited: Dietary laws and permissible actions for spiritual and social discipline.
  • Justice and Fairness: Standing up for justice even against oneself or relatives.
  • Obedience and Responsibility: Accountability for actions and obedience to Allah and His Messenger.
  • Relations with People of the Book: Encouragement for dialogue while inviting them to follow divine guidance.
  • Unity and Cooperation: Avoiding division, hypocrisy, and enmity; promoting righteousness.
  • Divine Reminders and Warnings: Lessons from past nations to highlight consequences of disobedience.

Overall, the surah guides the community toward ethical behavior, strong faith, and collective responsibility in accordance with divine law.
