Surah Al-Ma'idah – The Table Spread

Classification: Madani | Verses: 120

Surah Al-Ma’idah, revealed in Madinah, is the fifth chapter of the Quran. It emphasizes fulfilling covenants, obedience to Allah, and maintaining justice. The surah provides guidance on ethical, social, and religious responsibilities for the Muslim community.

  • Guides on lawful and prohibited actions, including dietary rules and proper conduct.
  • Encourages justice and fairness, even if it is against oneself or close relatives.
  • Stresses obedience to Allah and accountability for one’s actions.
  • Addresses the People of the Book, inviting them to follow true guidance while warning against hypocrisy.
  • Highlights unity, cooperation in righteousness, and avoidance of division within the community.
  • Reminds believers of lessons from past nations who disobeyed divine guidance.
  • Reinforces ethical living, strong faith, and collective responsibility according to Allah’s law.
