Surah Aal-Imran – The Family of Imran

Classification: Madani | Verses: 200

Surah Aal-Imran, revealed in Madinah, is the third chapter of the Quran. It emphasizes faith, unity, steadfastness, and trust in Allah. It addresses both Muslims and the People of the Book (Jews and Christians).

  • Highlights the Oneness of Allah and the truth of His revelations.
  • Discusses the stories of the family of Imran, including Maryam (Mary) and the birth of Isa (Jesus, peace be upon him).
  • Warns against distorting religion and rejecting divine guidance.
  • Encourages believers to remain firm during trials, especially referencing the Battle of Uhud.
  • Stresses generosity, patience, and reliance on Allah’s will.
  • Calls for unity in the Muslim community and dialogue with the People of the Book.
  • Ends by reminding believers to remain patient, strong, and conscious of Allah in all matters.
