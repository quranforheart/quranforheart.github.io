Surah At-Tahrim – The Prohibition

Classification: Madani | Verses: 12

Surah At-Tahrim, revealed in Madinah, addresses specific incidents involving the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) and his household, highlighting issues of personal conduct, accountability, and obedience to Allah. The surah reminds believers that actions contrary to divine guidance, even within the household of the Prophet, carry consequences. It emphasizes the importance of repentance, sincerity, and adherence to Allah’s commands. The chapter also praises those who remained steadfast in faith, such as the wives of the Prophet and other devout believers, providing lessons on devotion, caution against hypocrisy, and moral integrity.

  • Addresses personal conduct and accountability within the Prophet’s household.
  • Emphasizes the importance of obedience to Allah’s commands.
  • Highlights repentance, sincerity, and corrective action for mistakes.
  • Praises steadfast believers and devout members of the Prophet’s household.
  • Warns against hypocrisy, disobedience, and moral negligence.
  • Provides lessons on moral integrity, faithfulness, and adherence to divine guidance.
