Surah An-Nisa – The Women

Classification: Madani | Verses: 176

Surah An-Nisa, revealed in Madinah, is the fourth chapter of the Quran. It focuses on social justice, family laws, and the protection of the rights of women, orphans, and the vulnerable. It also provides guidance on governance, inheritance, and community relations.

  • Emphasizes justice, compassion, and protection of rights for women, orphans, and the weak.
  • Details rules of marriage, inheritance, and financial dealings to ensure fairness in society.
  • Addresses hypocrisy, betrayal, and those who oppose the Prophet (ﷺ).
  • Provides guidance on warfare, peace, and justice in conflict situations.
  • Encourages obedience to Allah, His Messenger, and those in authority.
  • Warns against hypocrisy and double standards in faith and action.
  • Concludes with principles of inheritance, clarifying distribution of wealth after death.
